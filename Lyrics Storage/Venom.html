ABOUT

by Genius

Venom is the main theme of the soundtrack for the 2018 Columbia Pictures movie Venom based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name.

The movie’s main character is a sentient alien Symbiote with liquid-like form, who requires a host to survive. The Symbiote bestows its powers upon the host, and the new dual-life form usually refers to itself as “Venom.”

The music video for Fall> includes several references to the Marvel character. Em is being chased by a liquid shadow, who ends up possessing him, and his eyes become entirely black. All of this, one can also see happening to Eddie Brock when he gets possessed and becomes Venom in the movie.
